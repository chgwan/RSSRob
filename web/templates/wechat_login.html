This needs your own registered 公众号 (WeChat Official Account). RSSRob lists any account's articles through your 公众号 backend — it logs in to mp.weixin.qq.com and uses its article-search API. A free 个人订阅号 is enough, and RSSRob only ever reads (it never posts to your account).
Don't have one? How to register
  1. Open mp.weixin.qq.com and click 立即注册 (Register).
  2. Pick 订阅号 (Subscription Account) — it's free. An individual (个人) subject type is fine and only needs a Chinese ID + a bank card / face scan to verify.
  3. Complete email + identity verification, then log in. You never have to publish anything — RSSRob only reads the backend's search/article APIs.
A 服务号 or company account also works, but a personal 订阅号 is the quickest free option.

Scan to open mp.weixin.qq.com, or just open it in this browser and log in.

After logging in, grab two things:

  1. token — it's in the address bar: …/cgi-bin/home?...&token=123456789. Copy just the number.
  2. cookie — the whole Cookie: request header:
    DevTools (F12) → Network tab → click any request to mp.weixin.qq.com → Headers → Request Headers → copy the entire Cookie value.
    ⚠️ Don't use document.cookie — the important login cookies are HttpOnly and won't show up there.
